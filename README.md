## Project Overview

This project presents a comprehensive business analytics solution for **New Peth Shoes Ltd.**, an SME-based online shoe retailer in Morpeth, Northumberland. The analysis identifies performance bottlenecks, regional inefficiencies, and growth opportunities through data-driven insights.

**Key Achievement**: Identified logistics issues causing 42% delayed shipments and provided actionable recommendations that could improve regional performance by 30-35%.

## Business Recommendations

### 1. Logistics Optimization
- **Implement regional distribution hubs** in South and West regions
- **Partner with premium logistics providers** for faster delivery options
- **Target**: Reduce late shipments from 42% to <20%

### 2. Regional Growth Strategy
- **Targeted promotions**: "South-West customer 10% discount"
- **Family engagement campaigns**: Bundle offers for underrepresented segments
- **Local partnerships**: Pop-up stores and community events

### 3. Sales Team Enhancement
- **Experience gap identified**: 3-4 years (South/West) vs 7-8 years (North/Midlands)
- **Training program**: 3-month mentorship with top performers
- **Incentive structure**: 5% commission above target for underperforming regions

### 4. Product Portfolio Optimization
- **Focus investment**: Expand Timberland and UGG inventory
- **Phase out**: Charles & Keith, Aldo (low ROI brands)
- **Seasonal strategy**: Strengthen summer/winter collections timing
